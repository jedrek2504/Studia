As the sun sets on the small town of Millfield, Indiana, a sense of unease settles over the community. For weeks now, strange occurrences have been happening--mysterious lights in the sky, bizarre animal behavior, and reports of missing persons. Despite the best efforts of local law enforcement, no one has been able to uncover the source of these phenomena. As tensions rise and the town becomes more and more isolated, a group of unlikely allies must come together to uncover the truth and save their community from the mysterious forces threatening it. But as they delve deeper into the mystery, they soon realize that some secrets are better left buried, and that the true cost of discovery may be more than they ever imagined.
